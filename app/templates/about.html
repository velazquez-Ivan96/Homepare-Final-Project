What is Homepare
Your favorite Home Adviser!

Homepare it`s an app thinked and designed, for those looking for the best price/value for their next home.

How it works?.

We scrapped the available houses in the Megalopolis ( CDMX - EDOMEX - Tlaxcala - Puebla - Morelos - Hidalgo) and create a DataBase on Heroku.

Save the economic datset of Megalopolis obtained by INEGI in Heroku.

The app will crossreference the information with the official economic data from INEGI so you can have the options of reviewing and compare between regions.

With the querys requested the app will deploy the information of each municipality so you can see and/or compare per municipality things like.

  • Average Price for Houses
  • Properties vailable
  • Average Price per M2
  • Average No. of Beedrooms
  • Development indexes, such as:
    • HDI
    • Income
    • Health
    • Education
  • Maps that will make you see in a bigger picture the data obtained.
